THE BIRTH OF COSMOS (THE COMPANY, NOT THE UNIVERSE)

OUR STORY

Cosmos was born out of one simple idea: learning about space shouldn’t feel like rocket science (even when it actually is). A handful of space-loving nerds came together [X years ago] to create a place where anyone could discover planets, stars, and galaxies in a fun, accessible way.

Over time, our little project grew into a thriving hub for cosmic news, guides, and stories that make the universe feel like your backyard. Today, our team still runs on equal parts curiosity, coffee, and stardust. And we’re just getting started—the universe is a big place, after all!

OUR TEAM

The Cosmos team is a mix of space nerds, science buffs, storytellers, and a few people who just really love staring at the night sky. Some of us are writers, some are educators, and some are designers—but all of us share the same mission: to make the wonders of outer space fun and accessible for everyone. We’re the kind of crew that gets equally excited about the latest rocket launch, a cool new image from a space telescope, or spotting a constellation on a clear night. Together, we collaborate with plenty of curiosity and humor to bring the universe a little closer, one story (and one star) at a time.
