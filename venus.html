VENUS

Type: Terrestrial Planet

Age: About 4.5 billion years old

Size: Diameter of about 12,104 km (7,521 miles)

Average Temperature: Around 465°C (869°F)

Fun Fact: Venus is the hottest planet in the solar system—even hotter than Mercury—because its thick atmosphere traps heat in a runaway greenhouse effect. It also rotates backwards compared to most planets, meaning the Sun rises in the west and sets in the east.
