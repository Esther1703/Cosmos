PLUTO

Type: Dwarf Planet

Age: About 4.5 billion years old

Size: Diameter of about 2,377 km (1,477 miles)

Average Temperature: Around -229°C (-380°F)

Fun Fact: Once considered the ninth planet, Pluto was reclassified as a dwarf planet in 2006. It has five known moons, the largest of which—Charon—is so big that the two actually orbit each other like a binary system.
