SUN

Type: G-type main-sequence star (Yellow Dwarf)

Age: About 4.6 billion years old

Size: About 1.39 million km in diameter—roughly 109 times wider than Earth

Average Temperature: Around 5,500°C (9,932°F) at the surface and more than 15 million°C (27 million°F) at the core

Fun Fact: The Sun makes up 99.8% of the total mass of our solar system and takes about 225 million years to orbit once around the Milky Way.
