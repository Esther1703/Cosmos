MARS

Type: Terrestrial Planet

Age: About 4.6 billion years old

Size: Diameter of about 6,779 km—roughly half the size of Earth

Average Temperature: Around −60°C (−80°F), though it can vary from −125°C at the poles to 20°C near the equator

Fun Fact: Known as the Red Planet for its iron-rich dust, Mars has the tallest volcano in the solar system—Olympus Mons, which is nearly three times the height of Mount Everest.
